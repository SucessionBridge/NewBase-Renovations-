What’s Included by Package

Here’s how Starter, Suite-Ready, and Luxury compare. Flooring is included in all packages (typical options below).

Starter Finish

Great for a clean rec room and storage.

  • Drywall, taping, prime & paint
  • Basic lighting & electrical (code-compliant)
  • Trim & doors
  • Flooring included: durable vinyl plank or carpet
  • Basic layout (no bathroom or kitchenette by default)
Suite-Ready (Most Popular)

Set up for a legal or in-law suite.

  • All Starter items
  • 1 bedroom + 3-pc bathroom
  • Kitchenette rough-in (plumbing & electrical)
  • Enhanced soundproofing between floors
  • Extra circuits & dedicated GFCI where required
  • Flooring included: vinyl plank (upgrade options available)
  • Egress guidance & permit/drawings support
Luxury

Premium finishes and custom details.

  • All Suite-Ready items
  • Custom millwork & feature walls
  • Glass shower & tile upgrades
  • Accent lighting & smart dimmers
  • Flooring included: premium vinyl plank, engineered wood, or tile
  • Higher-end fixtures & hardware
Notes on permits, egress & inspections
We build to Ontario Building Code and guide you through egress window sizing, ceiling clearances, and smoke/CO alarm rules. Permit drawings and inspection support are included with suite-ready scopes. Final approvals are issued by your local building department.
